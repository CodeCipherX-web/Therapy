Frequently Asked Questions

How is my data stored?
Your entries are stored in your Supabase project under the "mood_entries" and "chat_messages" tables. Only authenticated users can access their own entries. All data is encrypted and protected by Row Level Security policies.
Can I delete my data?
Yes — you can delete your account and all associated data. Contact support or use the account settings to manage your data. You have full control over your information.
Is this a replacement for professional help?
No. This tool is designed for self-tracking and general support. It is not a replacement for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing severe symptoms, please consult with a licensed mental health professional.
How do I use the mood tracker?
Simply rate your mood on a scale of 0-10, add an optional note about what influenced your mood, and save. You can view your history to track patterns over time. Sign in to save your entries.
Is the chat assistant AI-powered?
Currently, the chat uses simple pattern matching to provide supportive responses. In the future, this could be enhanced with AI capabilities through Supabase Edge Functions or third-party services.
